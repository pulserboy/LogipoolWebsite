Institute


The Logipool Infotech is a premier institution that offers quality education and training in It & Software, Design, Web Development, Data science, Mean & Mean Stack,Cyber Security,AWS etc.

The institute was established in 2016 with the vision of creating a hub of innovation and excellence in the field of technical education.

The institute also conducts research projects, workshops, seminars, and conferences on emerging topics and trends in technology.

The institute aims to produce competent and skilled professionals who can contribute to the technical development of the country and the world.



Why Prefer Logipool ?

We, as an institute do not believe in filling our batches with many students. Logipool gives more importance to the standard quality of teaching. We give students exposure to many technologies and ensure they are under the guidance of expert developers who have good IT industry experience.

What Makes us Different ?

Logipool not only sharpens students' hard skills but also emphasises on soft skills. A student is how to present in an interview, how to tackle tricky situations and how to groom themselves.
